Film

About

This film was made as part of the 30 Minute Film Festival for IM-UH 1011 Communications Lab at NYUAD. Through a combination of comedic and serious tones, our main goal was to convey the important role that water, especially freshwater, plays in our lives. Therefore, stay hydrated and don't waste water!

This 1.5 minute long film was made within 30 minutes, including planning, filming, and editing.
